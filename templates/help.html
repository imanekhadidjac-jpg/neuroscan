🎥 How To Use NeuroScan AI

About SHAP & Grad-CAM

SHAP explains which MRI features influenced the AI prediction. It helps doctors understand why the model predicted Alzheimer’s disease.

Grad-CAM highlights important brain regions directly on the MRI image. The colored heatmap shows the areas most relevant to the AI decision.

These Explainable AI techniques improve transparency, trust, and medical interpretation of deep learning predictions.
